The Science of Body Proportions

Why Data-Driven Fit?

Standard sizing labels (S, M, L) often fail because they don't account for individual proportions. A person with broad shoulders and a slim waist might need a different cut than someone with a narrower frame. FITME uses precise data to define your unique silhouette.

The Golden Ratio

Historically, artists used the 'Golden Ratio' to define beauty. In modern fashion, this translates to the 'Rule of Thirds'. By knowing your vertical and horizontal proportions, you can use clothing to create visual balance and height.

How to Measure Properly?

For the most accurate results, use a flexible tailor's tape. Keep the tape firm but not tight. Always measure while standing naturally in front of a mirror to ensure the tape stays level around your body.

Privacy Policy

Your privacy is our priority. FITME does not store your personal body data on any server. All measurements entered are stored temporarily in your local browser storage and are deleted when you clear your cache. We do not track individual users.
